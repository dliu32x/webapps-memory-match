Can you find all the matching pictures?

First, choose a level. The higher the level, the more picture cards you’ll get.

Then click a face-down card to peek at it. Click another to see if you’ve found a match. If not, they’ll flip back over and you can try again. If they do match, they’ll remain face up, and you’re getting closer to winning! If you get stuck, click Hint to uncover a match.

When you finish a game, you can click:
  • Replay to play that level again
  • Next Level to move up to more picture cards
  • Quit to go back to the beginning
